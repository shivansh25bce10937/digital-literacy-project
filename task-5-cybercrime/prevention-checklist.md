# Stay Safe Online: UPI Fraud Prevention Checklist
### Prepared by: Student Digital Ambassador

**Checklist to ensure that you stay safe online-**

* **The PIN Rule:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is strictly for authorizing outgoing payments.
* **Scanner Caution:** Do not scan any QR code sent to you by a stranger over WhatsApp or SMS, even if they claim it is a "payment voucher".
* **Transaction Headers:** Always read the transaction screen on your UPI app before hitting confirm to see if it says "Pay" or "Request".
* **Avoid Urgency:** Be skeptical of any "buyer" who creates artificial time pressure or claims a link will expire in minutes.
* **Identity Verification:** Whenever possible, stick to official campus groups and verify the identity of the person you are dealing with before sharing any digital details.
* **Immediate Reporting:** If you are defrauded, immediately report the incident to the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal at **cybercrime.gov.in**.
* **Emergency Contact:** Call the national cybercrime helpline at **1930** as soon as you realize a fraudulent transaction has occurred.
